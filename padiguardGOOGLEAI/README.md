# PadiSmart

## What is PadiSmart?
**PadiSmart** (formerly PadiGuard) is an integrated, mobile-responsive agricultural web application designed specifically to empower paddy farmers (*Petani Padi*) and farming communities (*Kelompok Tani*) in Indonesia. It bridges the gap between traditional farming and modern technology by providing a suite of smart tools—ranging from AI-powered disease detection to community resource scheduling—accessible right from a smartphone browser.

## The Purpose & Goal
The primary goal of PadiSmart is to **increase agricultural yields and economic stability** for farmers by solving three major root problems:
1. Widespread crop failure and pesticide misuse due to inaccurate disease diagnosis.
2. Inefficient water management and wastage.
3. Crashing market prices and equipment shortages caused by uncoordinated, simultaneous planting and harvesting within the same village.

By equipping farmers with accessible, data-driven tools, PadiSmart aims to foster a more resilient, efficient, and collaborative agricultural ecosystem.

## Key Benefits

### 1. Accurate & Instant Disease Diagnosis
* **Benefit:** Prevents massive crop losses and reduces chemical waste.
* **How it works:** Farmers can take a photo of a sick paddy plant. Using Gemini's Edge AI vision model, the app instantly recognizes 6 common conditions (such as Blast, Tungro, or Nitrogen Deficiency) and provides actionable, specific treatment advice in Indonesian.

### 2. Water Optimization & Cost Efficiency (Smart AWD)
* **Benefit:** Saves water (up to 15-30% reduction in water use) and lowers irrigation pumping costs without sacrificing yield.
* **How it works:** By fetching hyper-local 7-day weather forecasts, the system advises farmers when to irrigate and when to let the fields dry (Alternate Wetting and Drying method). It also prevents catastrophic drying during critical phases like flowering.

### 3. Price Protection via Coordinated Planting
* **Benefit:** Protects farmers from sudden price drops in grain (*gabah*) that occur when everyone harvests at the exact same time.
* **How it works:** The Community Sync dashboard maps out what varieties are planted and when. It auto-calculates harvest dates, allowing the community to stagger their planting schedules strategically.
